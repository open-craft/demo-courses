Polls contain a single question, letting users select from an array of answers for a choice that suits them best. When a user makes a selection and hits submit, their choice is sent to the server, and a tally of the results so far is presented to them. The top choice's percentage is highlighted while the user's selection is marked by a selected (but disabled) radio button on the side.
