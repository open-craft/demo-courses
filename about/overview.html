About This Course

This course will provide you with a brief overview of the Polls X-Block.

The Polls X-Block lets instructors put ungraded polls and surveys into thier cources so that students can provide feedback.

This XBlock has been contributed by McKinsey Academy, and is published under the AGPLv3 licence (see the LICENSE file). It has been developed by OpenCraft.

Requirements

To use Polls, Xblock-utils must be installed and the course must be set up to use it.
